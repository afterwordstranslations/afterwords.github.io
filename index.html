Our Team

Our team consists of three members, Angeliki, Chrysanthi and Anna Maria. We studied foreign languages, translation and interpretation at the Ionian University and after the end of our studies, we established our company Afterwords. By combining the skills and interests of each one of us, we are able to meet the needs and whatever is required in every translation project we undertake.

Aggeliki

If you need a technical translation, Aggeliki is the right person for the job. Patience and readiness are hard-to-find virtues, which explains why she set off to learn Chinese. When she is not capturing the meaning of a text, she captures moments in time with her camera.

Superpowers: English, German, Chinese, patience, readiness

Anna Maria

Anna Maria is keen on medical and psychological texts, but she never misses the opportunity to dive in a subtitling project. All the tasks she undertakes are conducted even better when accompanied by her awesome music collection.

Superpowers: English, German, French, communication skills, multi-tasking

Chrysanthi

Chrysanthi is our in-house linguist geek. She loves words even if they’re in legal terms, or boring financial statements. She’s always available to translate, even if she’s on a trip around the world!

Superpowers: English, German, Italian, Spanish, attention to detail, organize all the things
