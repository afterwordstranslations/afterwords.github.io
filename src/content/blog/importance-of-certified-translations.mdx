When dealing with official documents across borders, **certified translations** become essential. Whether you're applying for a visa, pursuing education abroad, or handling international legal matters, accurate and certified translations ensure your documents are accepted by authorities.

## What is a Certified Translation?

A certified translation is a translation accompanied by a signed statement of accuracy from the translator or translation company. This certification verifies that:

- The translation is complete and accurate
- The translator is qualified to translate the document
- The translation can be used for official purposes

## When Do You Need One?

**Immigration Documents**: Passport translations, birth certificates, marriage certificates, and academic transcripts often require certified translations for visa applications.

**Legal Proceedings**: Contracts, court documents, and evidence in international legal cases must be accurately translated and certified.

**Academic Purposes**: Universities and educational institutions typically require certified translations of transcripts and diplomas for admissions.
